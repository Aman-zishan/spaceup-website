ABOUT


What is
SpaceUp ?

SpaceUP is an open attendance space exploration unconference, where participants decide topics, schedule and structure of the event. In a SpaceUP event, there are no spectators, only participants. It is a 21st century global approach to space outreach. An 'unconference' is a loosely structured conference that mainly focuses on the informal exchange of information and ideas between participants, unlike usual conferences. The first SpaceUP event was held at the San Diego Air & space museum in 2010, as a 2-day event. In India, the first SpaceUP event was held in Bengaluru in 2012.

spaceup website

HOW
it works

There are no spectators at SpaceUp, only participants. All attendees are expected to give a demo, present a talk, or participate in a panel or roundtable. It's not as scary as it might sound, though. Sessions at SpaceUp are conversations, just like every conversation you've had (or wanted to have) at any other conference. The only difference is that the sessions are planned on the spot, which means we're sure to be talking about topics we find interesting.

Registration

On-site registration works a lot like any other conference; you'll pick up your badge, schedule, and maybe some goodies at the front desk.

T-5

T-5 talks are ignite-format talks. Participants give a presentation with slides, this is the format for you: each speaker gets 5 minutes to talk, with maximum 10 slides. (scheduled beforehand)

T-X

X (10-15) minutes sessions that can take the form of a talk, a Q&A, or a project demo (scheduled beforehand).

CUSAT



Established in 1971, Cochin University Of Science And Technology is a United Grants Commission approved State University situated in the heart of Kochi city, Kerala. It's primarily driven by the goal to promote research in applied science, technology, industry, commerce, management and social science for the advancement of knowledge and the betterment of society. Its motto is "Tejaswinavadhitamastu", which attributes to "may learning illumine us both, the teacher and the taught"

As an internationally known and reputed University, it's academics are run by diverse groups of School of- Engineering, Technology, Environmental Studies, Humanities, Law, Social Sciences, Medical Sciences & Technology, and Sciences; which are further divided into departments. Not just limited to academic schools, specialized centers of excellence have been established to foster and endorse greater research and development work. There are Centres for- Atmospheric Radar Research, Advanced Materials, Information Resource Management, Science Communication, Science in Society, Techno Strategic Studies, Social Exclusion & Inclusive Policy, Development of Marine Biotechnology, IPR Studies, Nanomaterials & Devices, Aquatic Animal Health Women’s Studies, Budget Studies, and Contemporary Studies, apart from a few more. It has also been accredited by the NAAC (National Assessment and Accreditation Council), AIU (Association of Indian Universities) and ACU (Association of Commonwealth Universities).
